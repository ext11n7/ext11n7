## About

I work at the intersection of product analytics, SQL research, and Python-based data work. My focus is simple: take messy logs, user behavior, and operational signals, then turn them into metrics, narratives, and decisions that actually help a product move.

I am currently studying Mathematical Foundations of Artificial Intelligence at Innopolis University and building a portfolio around product analytics, experimentation, and time-series analysis.

## What I Work With

`Python` `SQL` `PostgreSQL` `SQLite` `pandas` `numpy` `matplotlib` `seaborn` `Jupyter` `Product Metrics` `Funnels` `A/B Tests` `Time Series`
